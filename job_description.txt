**Job Title**: Machine Learning Engineer - Entry Level

**Company**: Elint AI Systems

**Location**: Remote (India)

**Job Description**:
We are looking for a passionate and entry-level Machine Learning Engineer to join our AI Innovation team. You will work closely with senior data scientists and software engineers to design, build, and deploy scalable machine learning solutions.

**Key Responsibilities**:
- Develop ML models for NLP and classification tasks.
- Clean, preprocess, and analyze large datasets.
- Collaborate on model deployment pipelines using Python.
- Work on open-source and internal projects related to computer vision or language models.

**Required Qualifications**:
- B.Tech/M.Tech in Computer Science, AI, Data Science, or related field.
- Strong understanding of supervised and unsupervised learning techniques.
- Proficiency in Python and ML libraries such as scikit-learn, pandas, NumPy.
- Familiarity with TensorFlow or PyTorch.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

**Preferred Skills**:
- Experience with NLP (spaCy, transformers).
- Version control (Git).
- Contribution to Kaggle or open-source projects.

**Nice to Have**:
- Knowledge of Docker, REST APIs.
- Awareness of LLMs like GPT, BERT, etc.
